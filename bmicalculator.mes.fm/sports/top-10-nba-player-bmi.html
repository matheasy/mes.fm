Top 10 NBA Players' BMI

Share:

Sports   •  Alex Stutz   •   August 06, 2014

Please note that BMI can be inaccurate for people who are fit or athletic because their high muscle mass can list them as overweight, even though their body fat percentages are relatively healthy. BMI is intended to give a general sense of a healthy body weight.

10. LaMarcus Aldridge


Image Source: http://thesource.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/06/aldridge.jpg

BMI: 24.5 (Normal Body Weight), Height: 6'11" (2.11 m), Weight: 240 lbs (108.9 kg)

LaMarcus Aldridge has really stepped up as an A player this past 2013-14 season and has shown what he is made of. He is one of the leaders on the team and has been accomplishing career highs in his last season. This guy is good! Look out for him this up and coming season.
